About Me

Hi! My name is Tom and I am currently taking a coding course alongside my full-time job. I am hoping to move into the coding industry to be able to combine a logical, problem solving mind with the freedom of creative design. I have a degree in Business Studies with a year in Professional Placement from Canterbury Christ Church University, and over 18 months experience working in the construction sector. In my spare time I enjoy playing football, golf, guitar, and recently took up Karate. Sometimes it's a struggle to fit everything in!


Why Coding?

I first got a taste for web design when working on a small start-up in Switzerland inbetween years at university (2017). This was a simple wordpress website that hit a brick wall when features we needed required coded solutions. Eventually the project came to a halt due to costs but this sparked an interest in web design that has stuck with me since. I was made aware of the Codecademy online course by a friend in November 2021 and decided to take on the challenge. I love the problem-solving approach that coding requires, and the satisfaction of finally completing a project and being able to see the fruits of your own labour.
